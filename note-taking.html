How we score

Every tool is rated 0–10 on a transparent scorecard across six weighted criteria. We add them up into one number — the blipradar score — and that score alone decides the ranking.

Output quality35%

How good the results actually are — accuracy, polish, and how often the output is usable without heavy fixing. Our single heaviest factor.

Value for money15%

What you get for the price, free tier included — whether it is worth it, not simply how cheap it is.

Ease of use15%

How quickly a new user gets productive — setup, learning curve, and everyday friction.

Momentum15%

Its trajectory right now — how fast it is shipping real improvements and gaining ground.

Reliability10%

Consistency you can count on — uptime, stable results, and how rarely it breaks or shifts under you.

Ecosystem10%

Everything around the tool — integrations, docs, community, extensions, and platform support.

Scores are re-checked every week and only change when something real changes. Rankings are merit-only — paid listings and affiliate links never affect a tool's position.
