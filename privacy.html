Privacy Policy

What we collect, why, and the choices you have.

Last updated 7 June 2026
This is a starting template to adapt for your jurisdiction, not legal advice. Have it reviewed by a qualified professional before you rely on it.

What we collect

  • Usage analytics — aggregate, non-identifying data about which pages are viewed, to improve the site.
  • Email — only if you subscribe to updates; used solely to send those updates.
  • Server logs — standard technical data (IP, browser, timestamps) kept briefly for security and diagnostics.

Cookies

We may use cookies for analytics and, on outbound affiliate links, third-party networks may set their own cookies to attribute a referral. You can block or clear cookies in your browser at any time.

Third parties

We share data only with the service providers that help run the site (for example, analytics and affiliate networks), each under their own privacy terms. We do not sell your personal information.

Your choices

  • Unsubscribe from emails using the link in any message.
  • Opt out of analytics via browser settings or "Do Not Track".
  • Request access to or deletion of your data by emailing us.

Children

blipradar is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their data.

Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be noted by the "last updated" date above.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email contact@blipradar.com.
